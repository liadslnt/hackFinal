Calculate area

Count detected pixels and convert them into km², creating a footprint signal that can be compared with trade growth.

What we can claim

The method estimates visible site footprint, not exact production. It helps test whether the physical site looks consistent with industrial ramp-up.

Why it matters

If trade volumes grow while the visible footprint stays flat, that becomes a stronger lead. If both grow together, the origin story becomes more plausible.
